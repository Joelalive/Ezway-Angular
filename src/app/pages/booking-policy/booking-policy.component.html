Booking Policy

1. Booking Process
Bookings can be made through phone or WhatsApp. Please follow these steps:

Contact Us: Reach out to our customer service through the provided channels.
Provide Details: Share your preferred vehicle, rental dates, and any specific requirements.
Payment Confirmation: A full payment is required to confirm your reservation. Your booking will be held once payment is processed.
2. Age Requirement
To rent a vehicle from Ezwaycar, you must:

Be at least 18 years old.
Hold a valid driving license.
For foreign nationals, a valid International Driving Permit (IDP) is also mandatory.

3. Identity Verification
At the time of vehicle pickup, you are required to:

Present original identification documents.
Provide a valid driving license.
Submit additional proof of identity for Indian nationals (e.g., Aadhaar card, voter ID).
4. Booking Confirmation
Upon successful payment, you will receive a confirmation message that includes:

Booking details
Vehicle information
Pickup location and time
5. Cancellation Policy
In case you need to cancel your booking, the following cancellation charges apply:

More than 24 hours before delivery: ₹500
4 to 24 hours before delivery: ₹1,000
Less than 4 hours before delivery: One day’s rent
Cancellations must be communicated through the same channel used for booking.
